Our story

Cirqle was founded in 2024 with a simple observation: brands spend billions on influencer marketing, yet everyday shoppers who organically share their purchases see nothing in return.

We flipped that. Cirqle connects brands with real customers who genuinely love their products — and pays those customers cashback for sharing. Everyone wins: brands get authentic content, shoppers get money back.

Launched in the UK, Cirqle now works with over 500 brands across fashion, food, tech, travel and more — from household names to exciting indie labels.
